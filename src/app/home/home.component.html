ABOUT US

Norton Industries, established in 1980 and headquartered in Ondipudur, Coimbatore, is a prominent Indian manufacturer and exporter specializing in pumps, motors, and control panels. Founded by engineering graduate Mr. N. Subramanian, the company initially focused on producing pumps for agricultural and domestic applications, as well as motors for industrial use. Over the years, Norton Industries has expanded its operations to include a cast iron foundry, Norton Ferro Castings, and a plastics division, Clear Plast, which manufactures PET preforms, bottles, and jars.
